
# Phase‑1 Notice & Legal Position

Apollo Care currently operates as a **health care cost‑sharing program** and is **not an insurance company**.  Membership involves voluntarily sharing medical expenses with other members; contributions are not premiums and there is no guarantee that a member’s claims will be paid.  Apollo strives to pay all valid claims on a best‑efforts basis, but the program is not subject to the same insurance regulations and guarantees.

## Legal Wrapper

The cost‑sharing program is administered by **Apollo Protocol LLC**, a Wyoming DAO LLC.  This legal wrapper provides limited liability protection and enables the DAO to sign contracts (e.g., with providers or reinsurers) while maintaining on‑chain governance.  Members are beneficiaries of the cost‑sharing arrangement; $APH token holders are the owners and decision‑makers of the DAO.

## Future Phases

Apollo intends to pursue regulatory pathways to offer fully licensed health insurance in future phases.  This may include obtaining sandbox insurance licences, partnering with existing carriers or forming a captive insurer.  Until such authorizations are obtained, the program remains a voluntary cost‑sharing ministry.  Any timelines or features described are aspirational and subject to DAO approval and market/regulatory conditions.
